Features

Everything your mosque display needs

A complete digital signage solution designed specifically for mosques, with zero monthly fees.

🕐

Automatic Prayer Times

Prayer times fetched daily from the Aladhan API using your mosque's zipcode and preferred calculation method. Includes Jummah time support.

📷

Image Slideshow

Rotate announcement images from a Google Drive folder. Images refresh automatically after each full rotation — update Drive, and the display updates itself.

▩

QR Codes

Display up to 4 QR code images with auto-generated labels. Perfect for donation links, event signups, social media, and WiFi access.

🏢

Multi-Tenant

Each mosque gets a unique ID and display URL. Multiple mosques can use the same platform with completely independent configurations.

📺

TV & Chromecast Ready

Optimized for 1080p and 4K displays with automatic TV detection, responsive scaling, wake lock to prevent sleep, and daily auto-reload at midnight.

⚙

Self-Service Admin

Sign in with Google, create your mosque, and configure everything — prayer settings, theme colors, Drive folder, and display intervals — from one dashboard.

How It Works

Up and running in three steps

No technical knowledge required. If you can use Google Drive, you can run this.

1

Create Your Mosque

Sign in to the admin dashboard with your Google account and fill in your mosque details, location, and prayer time preferences.

2

Add Your Content

Create a Google Drive folder with your slideshow images and QR codes. Paste the folder ID into the admin dashboard.

3

Display Anywhere

Open your unique display URL on any TV, Chromecast, tablet, or browser. It just works — fullscreen, auto-updating, always on.
