How mosque management uses it

1

Sign in & create your mosque

Use your Google account on the admin dashboard. Enter your mosque name, location, and prayer time preferences.

2

Add content to Google Drive

Create a shared folder with your announcement images and QR codes. Paste the folder ID into the dashboard.

3

Open on any screen

Load your unique display URL on a TV, Chromecast, or browser. It runs fullscreen, stays awake, and refreshes automatically.
