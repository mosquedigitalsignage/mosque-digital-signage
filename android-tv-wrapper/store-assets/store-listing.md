## App Name
Mosque Digital Signage

## Short Description (80 chars max)
Display prayer times, announcements, and slideshows on your mosque's TV.

## Full Description
Mosque Digital Signage turns any Android TV into a beautiful, always-on information display for your mosque.

Simply install the app, sign in with Google, and select your mosque. Your TV will automatically show:

• Accurate daily prayer times based on your location
• Rotating image slideshow from your Google Drive
• Community announcements managed by your admin
• QR codes linking to your mosque's website and donation page
• Inspirational Quran ayats and hadith in the footer

EASY SETUP
1. Create your mosque profile at mosquedigitalsignage.github.io/mosque-digital-signage/admin.html
2. Configure prayer times, upload images, and add announcements from the admin dashboard
3. Install this app on your Android TV, sign in, and select your mosque — done!
4. On future launches, your mosque loads automatically — no need to sign in again

FEATURES
• Multi-mosque support — manage multiple mosques from one account
• Sign in with Google — links your mosques to your account automatically
• Auto-updating prayer times — no manual adjustments needed
• Custom announcements — keep your community informed in real time
• Image slideshow — display event flyers, announcements, and photos from Google Drive
• QR codes — let visitors scan to reach your website or donate
• Custom ayats — replace default quotes with your own selections
• Link by UUID — easily connect to any registered mosque
• Stays on 24/7 — prevents screen sleep and auto-refreshes daily
• Completely free and open source

ADMIN DASHBOARD
Mosque administrators manage everything through a simple web dashboard — no technical skills required. Sign in with Google, create or link mosques, and your display updates instantly.

This app requires an internet connection to load mosque data and prayer times.
